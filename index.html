DETECTION SIGNATURES
  • ⊡
    SYMMETRY BREAK
    Panels wider than they look, trim that continues past a corner, wall sections that don't mirror their opposite
  • ◑
    SHADOW ANOMALY
    Shadows cast at wrong angles relative to light source, unexplained dark lines at floor or ceiling edges
  • ▦
    TEXTURE MISMATCH
    Paint finish change, grain direction break, newer drywall patch that doesn't match surrounding surface
  • ⬚
    DEAD SPACE
    Room interior smaller than exterior footprint suggests — unaccounted square footage hidden behind walls
  • ⊕
    HARDWARE TELLS
    Flush pulls, pivot hinges, magnetic catches, or odd fastener patterns visible in trim or paneling
  • ◎
    WEAR PATTERNS
    Floor scuffing arc near a wall, paint worn at edge height, baseboards with different dust accumulation
